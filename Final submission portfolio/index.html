About Me

My career goal is to become a capable communication designer.I am currently doing my bachelors in communication design from ISDI mumbai.its easy to be a designer but to be a good designer takes patience and and the desire to keep learning,i feel one never stops learning.five or six years down the line i would like to look back at my journey till now and be assured of the fact that ive grown.my goal as a designer would be to tell a story through my work, thats the beauty of design a good design can have a huge impact on how anything is percieved and i aspire to achieve this skill. i feel like creativity and being able to work in a team are much more important skills; developing these skills also teaches us how to be successful. learning new things/hobbies excites me i have always been into drawing or painting whiile always doodling on my sketchbook which is an organized mess of my ideas and drawings. traveling also is something I really love which is also why I also gained an interest for photography, always wanting a new challenge which is why design is something im passionate about, problem solving/ finding solutions are some of the skills I have which I would like to develop and become better.
